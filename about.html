About Open Integrated Curriculum

Open Integrated Curriculum is an open integrated curriculum platform designed to provide learners with an interdisciplinary and cross-domain learning environment. We believe that learning should not be limited to a single discipline, but should integrate knowledge from different disciplines in order to more comprehensively understand the world and solve problems.

At Open Integrated Curriculum, you will find courses covering various disciplines, including mathematics, physics, chemistry, biology, history, and programming. Our curriculum design emphasizes interdisciplinary integration. For example, you can learn relevant mathematics and programming knowledge while learning physics, so as to gain a deeper understanding of physical concepts.

Our goals are:

  • To provide an open and free learning platform where anyone can learn the knowledge of any discipline.
  • To break the boundaries between disciplines and provide interdisciplinary integrated courses to enable learners to understand the world more comprehensively.
  • To cultivate learners' critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

Welcome to Open Integrated Curriculum and explore the infinite possibilities of knowledge together!
